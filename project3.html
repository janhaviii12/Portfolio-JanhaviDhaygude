A Data-Driven Strategy for Cyclistic Bike-Share (Case Study)

Transforming Casual Riders into Loyal Members Using R, Excel & Tableau

Project Overview

In this case study, I explored usage patterns of casual vs. annual riders for Cyclistic, a Chicago-based fictional bike-share company, using real Divvy datasets from Q1 2019 and Q1 2020. As a junior data analyst, my goal was to derive insights that could help the marketing team convert more casual riders into annual members.

Data Analysis Approach

Using Excel for data cleaning, R for statistical analysis, and Tableau for visualization, I uncovered that casual riders take longer trips primarily on weekends, while members take shorter, more frequent rides during weekdays—indicating a commute-oriented pattern.

Key Findings

  • Usage Patterns: Casual riders prefer longer weekend trips, while members take shorter weekday rides
  • Peak Usage: Identified distinct peak usage trends by rider type and day of week
  • Behavioral Differences: Members show consistent weekday commuting patterns
  • Conversion Opportunities: Weekend-focused casual riders represent untapped potential

Technical Implementation

  • Data Source: Real Divvy datasets from Q1 2019 and Q1 2020
  • Data Cleaning: Excel for initial data preparation and validation
  • Statistical Analysis: R for pattern identification and statistical testing
  • Visualization: Tableau for creating interactive dashboards and charts

Key Outcomes

  • Peak Usage Trends: Identified peak usage trends by rider type and day
  • Marketing Strategy: Recommended a targeted marketing strategy including weekend perks, trial memberships, and behavior-based outreach
  • Business Growth: Helped translate data insights into business growth strategies

Impact & Recommendations

This project showcases my ability to blend analytical thinking, visualization, and stakeholder-aligned recommendations. The insights led to actionable marketing strategies that could significantly improve rider conversion rates and business growth for Cyclistic.
