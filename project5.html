Credit Operations Analytics

Overview

This project simulates a real-world Credit Operations Analytics system designed to support fintech lending teams in identifying early delinquency risk, improving data reliability, and reducing manual operational effort.

Using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI, the project focuses on credit risk monitoring, repayment behavior analysis, and SQL-driven prioritization logic—moving beyond static dashboards to production-aligned Credit Ops analytics.

Business Problem

  • Poor data quality across customer, loan, and repayment systems
  • Late identification of high-risk borrowers after repayment failures
  • Manual, non-scalable Credit Ops workflows
  • Lack of clear, interpretable risk metrics for Ops prioritization

These challenges lead to reactive decision-making, increased operational cost, and higher credit losses.

Project Objective

To improve credit operations decision-making by addressing data quality issues, enabling early delinquency detection, and reducing manual operational effort through Python- and SQL-driven analysis.

Technologies Used

  • Programming: Python (Pandas, Date Handling, Validation Logic)
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Visualization: Power BI (DAX, KPI Cards, Risk Dashboards)
  • Skills: Credit Risk Analytics, Data Cleaning, KPI Engineering, SQL Optimization

Dataset Structure

  • Customer Table: Credit profile and borrower segmentation
  • Loan Table: Loan exposure and status tracking
  • Payment Table: Repayment behavior and delinquency detection
  • Ops Action Table: Simulated Credit Ops interventions (SMS, calls, escalations)

Core Analytics & KPIs

  • Days Past Due (DPD) calculation and industry-standard DPD buckets
  • Delinquency Rate, Default Rate, On-Time Payment Rate
  • Repeat late payment detection and payment delay trends
  • Rule-based Early Warning Signals (EWS)
  • Manual vs automated Ops handling effectiveness

SQL Architecture

The project follows a modular SQL architecture with dedicated scripts for schema creation, risk metrics, roll-rate analysis, Ops effectiveness, and portfolio summaries.

  • DPD Engineering
  • Roll Rate Analysis
  • Early Warning Signal Generation
  • Ops Prioritization Logic

Power BI Dashboard

  • Portfolio Overview: Exposure, risk distribution, loan status
  • Delinquency Trends: Monthly DPD and roll rates
  • Early Warning System: High-risk loan identification
  • Ops Effectiveness: Collection performance by action type

Impact

  • Enabled early identification of high-risk loans before default
  • Reduced dependency on manual Ops decision-making
  • Created a scalable, automation-ready Credit Ops analytics framework
  • Demonstrated real-world application of SQL and Python in fintech risk analytics
