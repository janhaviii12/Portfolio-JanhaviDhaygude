About Me

Hi, I’m Janhavi — a recent graduate (2024) in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science from PES Modern College of Engineering. Right after graduation, I got an opportunity to work with Amazon as a CAP Associate on a contractual role, where I sharpened my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills in a fast-paced, customer-centric environment.

Currently, I’m actively seeking entry-level Data Analyst roles where I can apply and expand my skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, and Tableau. I enjoy turning data into meaningful insights that drive better decisions.

I would describe myself as calm, curious, and ambiverted — someone who thrives in both independent and collaborative spaces. My leadership journey began during my college days when I led my department as part of the NSS, organizing impactful initiatives and fostering strong team coordination.
